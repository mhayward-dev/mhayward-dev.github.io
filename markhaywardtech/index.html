I'm Mark, a software engineer based in London. I've been working in digital agencies for around 4 years now and I have a passion for making technology that changes people's lives.

This is why I have primarily focused on the charity sector whilst working in an agency. Through a well constructed serverside and elegant UI, I was amazed at how much enhanced technology could change the way a user interacts with your app.

In this way, charities have been able to reach a lot more donators and provide better financial support for those who are vulnerable and in need of vital care.

In whatever I decide to do in the future, my desire is to always push the boundaries with new and exciting technology.
