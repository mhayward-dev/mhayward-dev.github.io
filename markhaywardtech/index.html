I'm Mark, a software engineer based in London. I have over 10 years experience in this space, working in fullstack web development and also in native app development. Recently I have worked for 4 years in two digital agencies.

I have primarily focused on the charity sector whilst working in these agencies. It has been amazing to see the impact a well constructed serverside and elegant UI, can positively change the way a user interacts with your app.

In this way, charities have been able to reach a lot more donators and provide better financial support for those who are vulnerable and in need of vital care.

In whatever I decide to do in the future, my desire is to always push the boundaries with new and exciting technology.

I also enjoy learning the art of brewing great coffee, and I am a big music fan.
